About Lambda Labs

Lambda Labs is a software firm dedicated to the creation of useful programs. We are interested in moving the industry forward through the production of languages, libraries, tools, and research that help developers to reach better solutions.

Our Projects

We, as a whole and as individuals, have open-sourced many projects recently. These projects include a type-system for vanilla JavaScript, a testing suite for Java, a statistical performance assesser for JavaScript, an API test-suite, and a custom Lisp dialect.

All of these projects began within Lambda Labs to tackle a certain problem, and were then released into the public that other developers might benefit from them.

Our Products

We have made quite a few products, either for sale to consumers or to meet the needs of a client. Our earliest product was a gaming website, one which grew to over ten-thousand daily views. We then entered into the app market with tools for math and chemistry, as well as a tool for saving media to the cloud. We continue to create new websites and apps for various platforms including iOS, Android, and Mac.

For our clients, we have made a social network, a geolocation app, and some education apps adopted by the local high school diocese. Additionally, we have made ecommerce websites and business tools for our clients.

Our Research

We have conducted research, mostly into the areas of language interpretation and type-systems, to further our understanding of the field. Additionally, when we have made tools which closely neighbored research, we have taken the time to formalize the information into papers.
